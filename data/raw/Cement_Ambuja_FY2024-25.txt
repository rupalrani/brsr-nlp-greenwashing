Zero Liquid Discharge: implemented at ALL plant locations. No wastewater discharged. Board training: 87.5% of Board Directors covered on Business Strategy, Finance, Governance, HR, Industry topics; 62.5% on ESG Landscape; 37.5% on ESG & Climate topics (detailed breakdown of training topics with percentages is among the most granular governance disclosure in this corpus).

Key ESG context: Company is India's second-largest cement maker. Sustainability disclosures include Adani Foundation CSR governance layer. Designated Consumers (PAT scheme): Maratha, Suli, Rauri, Ambujanagar, Ropar, Rabariyawas, Bhatapara, Sankrail -- all achieved PAT Target EXCEPT Suli and Rauri (which is a notably honest disclosure of non-achievement).

Human rights and supply chain: Anti-Corruption Policy, Supplier Code of Conduct, statutory compliance checks at entry. POSH mechanism with Internal Committee.

Specificity note: PAT non-achievement disclosure and the zero-liquid-discharge confirmation are among the highest-specificity disclosures in the Cement sector. The Board training breakdown by percentage topic is unusually granular.
