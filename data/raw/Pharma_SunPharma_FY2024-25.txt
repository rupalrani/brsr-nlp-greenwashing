Key ESG officer: Mr. Aalok Shanghvi, Whole-time Director & COO, responsible for sustainability decisions. Board P6 policy: only internally reviewed (no external assessment of P6 policies). This is a transparency gap -- unique in this corpus.

Quantified targets: Scope 1+2 emissions: 35% reduction by 2030 vs 2020 baseline (earlier commitment; the FY24-25 BRSR supersedes this with a "specific intensity" metric). Hazardous waste co-processing: 47% achieved in FY24-25 vs 30% target. Water consumption target: 10% reduction by 2025. 

Diversity target: 30% women in global workforce by 2040.

Recognition: Top 5% of global pharmaceutical companies in S&P Global Sustainability Yearbook 2025. Recognised among Financial Times Asia-Pacific Climate Leaders 2024 (2nd consecutive year).

Specificity note: The co-processing achievement (47% vs 30% target) is the clearest target-vs-outcome disclosure in the Pharma sector. The 30% women target with a 2040 horizon, while time-bounded, is a long horizon. The absence of external P6 policy assessment is a gap worth flagging.
