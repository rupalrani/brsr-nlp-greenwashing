Leadership statement (from filing): the company describes decarbonisation as "a deliberate choice with shared enthusiasm" (paraphrased). The report describes moving "from intent to impact" (quoted phrase under 15 words) and highlights AI-enabled systems, digitalisation, and automation.

Transition context: Steel is the hardest-to-abate sector in the corpus. The restructuring of UK and Netherlands operations for green steelmaking (electric arc furnaces) is the dominant ESG story for Tata Steel globally and directly material to its BRSR disclosures.

Specificity note: The restatement of FY23-24 comparatives with reasons given (boundary change + ISF guidelines Dec 2024) is among the more transparent methodological disclosures in this corpus. The named industry standard changes (SEBI Circular SEBI/HO/CFD/PoD2/CIR/P/0155 dated 11 Nov 2024) provide an audit trail.
