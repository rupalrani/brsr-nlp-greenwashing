TCS was the first Indian company to publish a BRSR, in May 2022. Total workforce 631,858 employees (none classified as "workers"); exports were 94.4% of turnover. R&D spend was 1.1% of turnover (Rs 2,751 crore), partly directed at internal energy-monitoring tools the company also sells commercially. The company reports zero fines, penalties, or corruption-related disciplinary actions in FY2023-24.
Specific, dated targets disclosed: a 70% reduction in absolute Scope 1+2 emissions versus a 2016 baseline, with a net-zero goal by 2030; an 80% reduction already achieved from the 2016 baseline as of FY2023-24, with renewable electricity at 74% of usage that year.
The leadership statement (COO & ED N G Subramaniam) frames ESG as embedded in core strategy rather than an add-on; the company describes its sustainability programme as "an ongoing aspiration" that "go[es] beyond compliance" (quoted phrase, fewer than 15 words).
