Assurance: DNV Business Assurance India Private Limited, Reasonable (BRSR Core) + Limited (remaining). Listed on NSE, BSE, and NYSE (US-listed -- highest disclosure bar in the corpus).

Sustainability purpose: "accelerating access to affordable and innovative medicines" -- mission-linked, not just a reporting exercise. Goal of serving 1.5 billion patients by 2030.

Key ESG metrics (FY2025): Scope 1+2 emissions reduced by 33% vs FY2023 baseline. Energy-saving initiatives avoided 8,073 tonnes CO2 and saved 7.75 million kWh in FY25. Scope 3 emissions intensity reduced by 31% since FY2023 (supply chain). Waste reduction: 20-22% across 9 high-volume products. Life Cycle Assessment conducted for 2 API products.

Governance: ESG objectives embedded in MD performance scorecard; Board + NRC formally approve targets. This is among the strongest governance integration in the corpus.

Supplier ESG: 1,761 vendors (205 critical) confirmed alignment to Supplier Code of Conduct in FY25. 37 vendor PSCI audits covering ~INR 500 crore yearly spend. CAPA implementation tracked annually.

Key context: NYSE-listed, US SEC reporting requirements create an incentive structure for more rigorous non-financial disclosure compared to BSE/NSE-only filers -- relevant context for the greenwashing analysis.
